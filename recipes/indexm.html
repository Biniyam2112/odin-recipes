egg salad

Egg salad is a timeless and versatile dish that can be enjoyed in a variety of ways.
It's perfect for sandwiches, wraps, or even on its own as a light meal.
This recipe combines hard-boiled eggs, creamy mayonnaise, and a touch of mustard for a simple yet delicious flavor.
The addition of celery and onion adds a satisfying crunch, making it a perfect balance of textures.
Whether you're looking for a quick lunch or a potluck favorite, this egg salad is sure to please.

    Ingredients

  • 6 hard-boiled eggs, chopped
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon yellow mustard
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped celery
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped onion
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • A pinch of paprika

    Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the chopped hard-boiled eggs, mayonnaise, and mustard
  2. Add the finely chopped celery and onion to the bowl.
  3. Gently mix all the ingredients until well combined.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. If desired, sprinkle a pinch of paprika on top for a touch of color and flavor.
  6. Chill the egg salad in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before serving.
  7. Serve the egg salad on bread, in a wrap, or on a bed of lettuce. Enjoy!
